01  Case Study

Connecting learners with experts through real-time assistance.

A platform where learners meet experts for live sessions, coaching, and a way to earn from their skills. I designed and built the whole web side of it, from how people find experts to how they build trust and run sessions in real time.

UI/UX Design Frontend Development AI Integration
Built with Angular Spring Boot Tailwind Figma Illustrator

02  Context & Challenge

Finding the right expert should feel simple.

Right now, learners have a hard time finding the right expert, trusting them enough to book, and keeping the conversation in one place. Odidact takes those problems off the table so learning is easy from the first step.

Discovery friction

Finding the right expert takes too much time across multiple platforms.

Low trust before booking

There's not much to go on, so it's hard to judge expertise or credibility before you commit.

Fragmented communication

Conversations get scattered, and without any structure people get confused and drop off.
